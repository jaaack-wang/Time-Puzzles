# Fact Types Summary

| Fact Name | Level | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `YearRangeFact` | Year | The year is within a randomly generated 100-year range. |
| `DayOfYearFact` | Day | Specifies the ordinal day of the year (e.g., "100th day of the year"). |
| `WeekdayInYearFact` | Day | Specifies the Nth occurrence of a weekday in the year (e.g., "3rd Monday of the year"). |
| `WeekdayInMonthFact` | Day | Specifies the Nth occurrence of a weekday in the month (e.g., "2nd Friday of the month"). |
| `DayOfMonthFact` | Day | Specifies the exact day of the month, or if it is the first/last day. |
| `ExplicitYearFact` | Year | Explicitly states the year (e.g., "The year is 2025"). |
| `DecadeFact` | Year | Specifies the decade the year belongs to (e.g., "The 1990s"). |
| `WeekdayFact` | Day | Specifies the day of the week (e.g., "It is a Monday"). |
| `MultiWeekdayFact` | Day | Specifies that the day is one of a set of weekdays (e.g., "Monday or Tuesday"). |
| `DayOfMonthRangeFact` | Day | Specifies if the day is before or after a certain day of the month. |
| `MonthFact` | Month | Specifies the month of the year. |
| `LeapYearFact` | Year | States that the year is a leap year. |
| `SeasonFact` | Month | Specifies the season (Winter, Spring, Summer, Autumn) based on the month. |
| `ChineseZodiacFact` | Year | Specifies the Chinese Zodiac animal for the year. |
| `LunarMonthFact` | Month | Specifies the month according to the Chinese lunar calendar. |
| `WesternZodiacFact` | Month | Specifies the Western Zodiac sign (e.g., Aries, Taurus). |
| `PersonAliveFact` | Year | States that a famous person (e.g., Kobe Bryant, Steve Jobs) is alive during this time. |
| `USPresidentFact` | Year | States that a specific US President is in office. |
| `EventFact` | Year | States if it is a Summer Olympic year or a FIFA World Cup year. |
| `KnowledgeBaseEventFact` | Variable | Relates the date to a historical event from a knowledge base (same year, month, or day). |
